Products   High Molecular (HM)
 
High molecular (HM) is a high performance polymer having low coefficient of friction, good abrasion resistance, good chemical resistance etc. It is used in shipbuilding, textile industries and also in biomedical applications. The Melting-point of High Molecular lies between 120 and 150 C. It is the most widely used plastic industries. Its primary use is within packaging.  Benefits:
  • It is highly resistant to corrosive chemicals, with exception of oxidizing acids.
  • It has extremely low moisture absorption
  • Low coefficient of friction
